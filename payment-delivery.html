Self Collection

You are most welcome to collect your order from my studio by appointment, please see Contact Us for details. Cakes & Gateaux by L'event Plan cannot accept liability for any damage caused to your order once it has left the premesis.

Personal Delivery

Alternatively, personal delivery of wedding and party packages is available on request. Delivery is free for customers within Lagos, outside this area delivery is charged per mile.

Shelf Life

As we do not add preservatives to any of our products to ensure that they are at their best we suggest that all our cakes and biscuits are consumed within 48 hours. Fruit cake will last much longer.
