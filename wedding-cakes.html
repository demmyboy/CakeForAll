Wedding Cakes

View Our Menu

At Cakes & Gateaux by L'event Plan, we allow an initial design consultation and tasting session before your wedding day where you can taste up to 3 flavours of your choice package for customers within Lagos; Nigeria . For consultations outside this area,Conditions apply Alternatively, couples are welcome to visit my studio by appointment.

All cakes are filled and iced, include some simple decoration and are finished with a ribbon on an iced cake board. Tiers are stacked or separated as desired or displayed on a stand. Extensive decoration is subject to additional cost.



Cakes & Gateaux by L'event Plan has been tried,tested and trusted to help make your wedding day memorable!
