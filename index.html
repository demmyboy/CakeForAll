Welcome To
Cakes & Gateaux by L'event Plan

My name is Lara, CEO Cakes & Gateaux by L'event Plan based in Lagos,Nigeria. A graduate of Mass Communication from The lagos State University in Lagos, Nigeria. I have always had the love for events and passion for baking so i somehow knew i wasnt going to practise what i studied but practise what i love doing, then i immediately undertook a hygiene certification which was a great step to my lifelong ambition to start my own bakery world.

Cakes & Gateaux by L'event Plan is a fully insured and registered company incharge of event from birthdays,weddings, baby and bridal shower and special occassions , And we also organise cake trainning courses at different times of the year for those who to do what we do.

I started the business solely as the only staff, now we have a growing staff strenght which helps to improve the standard of the business. Cakes & Gateaux by L'event Plan started full business in May,2011 and hope to be one of the best cake and event company in few years to come
